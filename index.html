Our Story

A child's love that became a mission

Hi, I'm Brandon! I started this initiative with my mom back in 2018 when I was still in elementary school. My great-grandmother suffered from breast cancer and passed away before I ever had the chance to meet her.

We started this project in her memory and in honor of all the incredible women we know who have endured this disease and its treatment. Our dog Dash helps out too!

Since then, I've seen breast cancer touch even more friends and family — including my grandma in 2024, and my mom in 2025. Thankfully, they are both doing well now. That only deepens our commitment to this work.

Together, we partner with Mount Sinai Comprehensive Cancer Center in South Florida, delivering baskets to patients and taking a data-driven approach to continually improve what we include.

What We Give

Inside every basket

Each basket (often a backpack for added utility) is thoughtfully curated with products that ease the side effects of treatment and bring a little joy to a difficult day.

🎒

Chemotherapy Care Basket

A full backpack packed with comfort essentials for patients going through chemo treatment.

  • Aluminum-free deodorant
  • Alcohol-free mouth rinse
  • Silky scarf
  • Comfy socks
  • BPA-free water bottle
  • Tissues & wet wipes
  • Inspirational notebook
  • Stress ball
  • Gum & pen
  • Blanket
  • Chapstick
  • Hard candy
  • Organic ginger tea
  • Notes of encouragement

Note: Contents may change based on information gathered from our patient surveys.

🌿

Radiation Care Bundle

A targeted collection of products specifically proven to protect and soothe skin during radiation therapy — a new addition launched in 2026.

  • Dove soap
  • Aloe vera gel
  • Aveeno cream
  • Aquaphor

Why it matters: After chemo, most breast cancer patients face weeks of daily radiation, which causes painful skin irritation. This bundle directly addresses those challenges.
